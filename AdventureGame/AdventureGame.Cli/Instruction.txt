Adventure Game – Player Instructions
Objective:
Explore the room, defeat monsters, collect potions and weapons, and reach the exit.

Controls:
W - Move Up
S - Move Down
A - Move Left
D - Move Right

Tile Types:
.	Empty floor — move freely
#	Wall — cannot pass
P	Player start position
E	Exit — finish the level (only if all monsters defeated)
M	Monster — triggers a battle
I	Potion — restores 10 health
S	Weapon — increases attack power by 10

Battles use a number guessing mini-game:
Guess a number between 1–10.
Outcome depends on your guess vs. the monster’s guess.

Battle Outcomes:
Perfect hit - defeat monster instantly
Player hits - monster loses health
Monster hits - player loses health
Both counter - nothing happens

Player loses if health drops to 0.
Player wins if monster’s health reaches 0.

Tips
Pick up potions (I) to stay alive.
Upgrade attack power (S) to defeat monsters faster.
You cannot exit (E) until all monsters are defeated.
The room is bordered by # — walls block movement.

Winning:
Defeat all monsters and move to the exit (E) to complete the level.

Losing:
If your health reaches 0 in battle, the game ends.
